Manufacturing process: JuteWorks sources high-quality jute fibers from local farmers and processes them into yarn. The yarn is then woven into fabric and cut and sewn into bags by skilled artisans. The bags are finished with sturdy handles and other custom details.

Marketing and distribution: JuteWorks markets its bags through trade shows, social media, and its website. The bags are sold online and in select retail stores. JuteWorks also works with corporate clients to create custom bags for events and promotions.

Sustainability: JuteWorks is committed to sustainability and eco-friendliness. All of its bags are made from natural jute fibers, which are biodegradable and compostable. JuteWorks also uses eco-friendly dyes and printing processes to minimize its environmental impact.

Social responsibility: JuteWorks is committed to supporting local communities and empowering women. The company provides fair wages and safe working conditions for its artisans, many of whom are women. JuteWorks also supports local charities and community projects.
