Manufacturing process: EcoCandles uses natural soy wax and beeswax, which are renewable and biodegradable. The wax is melted and blended with high-quality fragrance oils and natural dyes. The mixture is then poured into reusable glass jars and finished with cotton wicks.

Marketing and distribution: EcoCandles markets its products through its website, social media, and select retail stores. The company also offers custom candles for weddings, corporate events, and other special occasions. EcoCandles ships its products nationwide.

Sustainability: EcoCandles is committed to sustainability and eco-friendliness. The company uses natural, renewable materials and avoids harmful chemicals and synthetic fragrances. EcoCandles also uses recycled and recyclable packaging and encourages customers to reuse or recycle their glass jars.

Social responsibility: EcoCandles is committed to supporting local communities and ethical business practices. The company sources its materials from sustainable and fair trade suppliers. EcoCandles also donates a portion of its profits to environmental and social causes.
