PRIVACY POLICY

Introduction

WORK PATH is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of personal information provided by job applicants seeking employment opportunities for semi-literate.

Information Collection and Use

We collect personal information from job applicants solely for the purpose of evaluating their suitability for employment opportunities. This information may include but is not limited to:

  • Name
  • Contact information (email, phone number, etc.)
  • Other relevant job-related details

The collected data will be used strictly for recruitment purposes and will not be shared with third parties without the applicant's explicit consent.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard the personal information of job applicants from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Retention Period

The personal information of job applicants will be retained for the duration necessary to assess their eligibility for employment opportunities. After this period, the data will be securely disposed of.

Consent

By submitting your application and providing personal information, you consent to the collection, use, and processing of your data in accordance with this privacy policy.
